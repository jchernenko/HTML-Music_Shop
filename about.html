Welcome to Sunrise Music Shop, where music enthusiasts and collectors alike can find a wide variety of classic and contemporary albums in their original analog form. Our store is a haven for those who appreciate the warm, rich sound of vinyl, and we pride ourselves on curating a diverse selection of genres and artists to suit every taste.

Whether you're a seasoned collector looking to add to your collection, or a music lover new to the world of vinyl, our knowledgeable staff are on hand to offer advice and recommendations, and to help you find the perfect record for any occasion. From rare and hard-to-find releases, to new releases and reissues, we are passionate about sharing the magic of vinyl with our customers.

So come and browse our shelves, discover your new favorite artist, and experience the unique pleasure of listening to music on vinyl. We look forward to welcoming you to our store and sharing our love of music with you.
